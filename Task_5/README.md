# üìä Tableau Project ‚Äî Sales & Profit Insights Dashboard  

This project presents a **comprehensive Tableau dashboard** built using the **Superstore dataset**, designed to explore and analyze key business metrics such as **sales, profit, quantity, and growth trends** across various **categories, sub-categories, regions, and time periods**.  

The project focuses on building **interactive and dynamic visualizations**, helping to identify **top-performing products, regions, and categories**, as well as areas needing improvement.  
Through calculated filters, running totals, and conditional formatting, the dashboard provides actionable insights into overall business performance.  

---

## üß≠ Project Overview  

The dashboard includes multiple Tableau worksheets that collectively showcase:  
- **Category and Sub-Category analysis** by sales and profit  
- **Year-over-year growth** in profitability  
- **Region-wise and Month-wise trends**  
- **Dynamic interactivity** using filters and sliders for deep-dive exploration  
- **Data source filters** to refine the dashboard to specific product segments

## üéØ Key Tasks Performed  

1. **Top 10 Profit-Making Subcategories** ‚Äî Identified and visualized with associated categories.  
2. **Revenue Filter (200K‚Äì250K)** ‚Äî Applied to ensure analysis focuses on relevant performing segments.  
3. **Month-wise Profit Analysis** ‚Äî Created dynamic visual controlled by **year slider** for interactivity.  
4. **Category & Subcategory Wise Sales** ‚Äî Filtered where revenue > 200K and profit > 20K.  
5. **Region-wise Profit Analysis** ‚Äî Enhanced bars with category, sales, and profit values.  
6. **Subcategory Quantity & Average Profit** ‚Äî Displayed in bar charts for operational insight.  
7. **Formatted Profit Tables** ‚Äî Customized tables with dark backgrounds, highlighted headers, and non-banded rows.  
8. **Conditional Formatting** ‚Äî Used to mark top and bottom profit-generating subcategories dynamically.

## üí° Key Insights  

- **Technology** dominates profitability, particularly in *Phones* and *Copiers*.  
- *Furniture* exhibits high sales but low profitability due to losses in *Tables* and *Bookcases*.  
- *Office Supplies* has moderate performance, with *Binders* and *Storage* as steady contributors.  
- Yearly profit growth shows a consistent upward trajectory from **2014 to 2017**.  
- Profit fluctuations across months emphasize the need for **inventory and discount optimization**.
